My Top 10 Favorite Songs

Eminem - Killer (Remix) ft. Jack Harlow, and Cordae

A song by American rapper Eminem from the deluxe edition of his eleventh studio album Music to Be Murdered By. The song was released as the album's thirteenth track on December 18, 2020 via Shady Records along with the rest of Music to Be Murdered By - Side B.

Eminem - Godzilla ft. Juice WRLD

A song by American rapper Eminem featuring American rapper Juice Wrld. It was released as a single on January 28, 2020, from Eminem's eleventh studio album Music to Be Murdered By (2020).

Pentakill III: Lost Chapter - Lost Chapter

The first song that was performed by the returning rock band Pentakill in a virtual concert in YouTube on September 9, 2021.

NF - The Search

The Search is the fourth studio album by American rapper NF. It was released on July 26, 2019, through NF Real Music and Caroline, and was preceded by the singles "Why", "The Search", "When I Grow Up" and "Time".

Lil Nas X - Industry Baby

A song by American rappers Lil Nas X and Jack Harlow, released on July 23, 2021, through Columbia Records. The song features co-production by American rapper and record producer Kanye West and production duo Take a Daytrip.

Pelikula - Janine Teñoso feat. Arthur Nery

Janine Teñoso and Arthur Nery came together to release a new single, "Pelikula", which speaks about the story of a couple and their distant feelings for each other. The retro, easygoing song was released on Friday, July 2, under Viva Records. Janine shared that she wrote the chorus for "Pelikula" with Arthur back in March 2020 at the outset of the pandemic lockdown.

Russ - Missin You Crazy

A song written and performed by Russ and Aired in YouTube on December 13, 2018.

Cueshé - Ulan

Cueshé is a Filipino pop rock band from Cebu, Philippines, currently based in Manila. They performed this song and aired it in YouTube on October 26, 2009.

Hale - The Day You Said Goodnight

Hale is the self-titled debut album by OPM Filipino band Hale, released on April 2005 under EMI Philippines. It was certified Triple Platinum (90,000 copies sold) on May 2006. The song "The Day You Said Goodnight" was aired in YouTube on November 7, 2012.

Mayonnaise - Synesthesia

Mayonnaise is a five-piece Filipino alternative rock band. The band is fronted by Monty Macalino and is famous for winning the "Red Horse Muziklaban" in 2004. The song was a part of an album called "Pula" in 2005. The song was aired in YouTube on February 7, 2011.
